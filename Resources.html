Resources

- Beyond Blue

Beyond Blue is an Australian based mental health company who strive to reduce the impact of mental health based stigma and discrimination as well as provide affective support to those who might not otherwise have access.

www.beyondblue.org.au

- Headspace

Headspace is a counselling service with locations all around Australia targeted towards people aged 12-25 they offer in person and low cost phone sessions for people in rural areas.

headspace.org.au

Crisis Helplines

- Life line

Life line is the national suicide hotline to be used in emergency situations for suicide prevention where you can call as well as text.

13 11 14 OR www.lifeline.org.au
